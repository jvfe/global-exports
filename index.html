Vega-Lite Global Exports Network

Data acquired from the UN database.

Check out an even better version on Observable


  • An interactive visualization of exports among countries, taking into account major trading partners of each country.
  • Edge between country x and country y means that a meaningful part of country x's exports go to country y, therefore, country y is a 'major trading partner' of country x.
  • Edge width corresponds to the percentage of that country's exports that go to the other country, the thicker it is, the larger the percentage of the exports.
  • Country node size corresponds the in-degree of the country, that is, how many countries have said country as one of their 'major trading partners.
